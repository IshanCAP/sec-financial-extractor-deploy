**STOCK OPTIONS / EQUITY AWARDS**
- Look for: "stock options", "option activity", "outstanding options", "exercisable", "vested"
- Tables showing: "Options Outstanding", "Weighted Average Exercise Price", "Shares Available"
- Extract: outstanding shares, vested/unvested split, weighted average exercise price (WAEP)

**RSUs (Restricted Stock Units)**
- Look for: "restricted stock", "RSU", "unvested shares", "restricted awards"
- Tables showing: "Restricted Stock Activity", "Unvested Shares"
- Extract: outstanding/unvested shares (RSUs have NO exercise price)

**PSUs (Performance Stock Units)**
- Look for: "performance shares", "PSU", "performance-based", "target shares", "maximum payout"
- Extract: target and maximum share amounts

**WARRANTS**
- Look for: "warrant", "common stock purchase rights", "pre-funded", "exercise price"
- Extract EACH tranche separately (Series A, Series B, pre-funded, etc.)
- For "nominal" or "de minimis" exercise prices â†’ use 0.0001

**CONVERTIBLE DEBT / NOTES**
- Look for: "convertible notes", "convertible debt", "conversion price", "conversion ratio"
- Tables showing: principal amount, conversion rate, shares issuable upon conversion
- Extract: principal, conversion price, conversion shares

**CONVERTIBLE PREFERRED STOCK**
- Look for: "preferred stock", "conversion ratio", "convertible into common"
- Extract: shares outstanding, conversion ratio

**SARs (Stock Appreciation Rights)**
- Look for: "stock appreciation rights", "SAR", "phantom stock"
- Extract: outstanding SARs, exercise price

**ESPP (Employee Stock Purchase Plan)**
- Look for: "employee stock purchase", "ESPP", "shares reserved", "purchase plan"
- Extract: shares available or reserved

**OTHER DILUTIVE INSTRUMENTS**
- Anything that says "exercisable into common stock"
- Anything that says "convertible into common shares"
- Rights, units, earnouts tied to share issuance
